Specify details about the rail track gauge.
  • The rail track gauge shall be 1676 mm.

 

  • Single Phase auto transformers of 765kV class and 3-Phase auto transformers of 400kV class shall have four (4) rails and other voltage class transformers shall have two (2) rails.

 

  • However, Generator transformers of 765kV & 400kV class (single phase units) may have two (2)/three (3) rails
